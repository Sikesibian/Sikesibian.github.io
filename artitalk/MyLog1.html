构建安全系统的设计方式

  • The Trusted Computing Base(TCB)

    TCB设计模式
    • Unbypassable
    • Tamper-resistant
    • Verifiable
      Keeping the TCB simple and small is an excellent practice.
      TCB的优点
    • Know what is in the TCB
    • Try to make the TCB unbypassable, tamper-resistant, and as verifiable as possible
    • Keep It Simple, Stupid (KISS)
    • Decompose for security

  • TOCTTOU Vulnerabilities

  • Leveraging Modularity

构建安全系统的原则

  • Security is economics

  • Least privilege

  • Use fail-safe defaults

  • Separation of responsibility

  • Defense in depth

  • Psychological acceptability

  • Human factors matter

  • Ensure complete mediation

  • Know your threat model

  • Detect if you can’t prevent

  • Don’t rely on security through obscurity

  • Design security in from the start

  • Conservative design

  • Kerkhoff’s principle

  • Proactively study attacks
