About Me

Hey There! This is Sikesibian/Sike, a junior undergrad majoring Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) at University of Chinese Academy of Science. PQC is the study and development of cryptographic systems that are secure against both classical and quantum computational threats, preparing for a future where quantum computers might break current encryption methods. I’m also a member of NeSE .
